Mojang Studios

Mojang Studios is the Swedish video game developer that now owns and maintains Minecraft after Microsoft acquired them in 2014 for $2.5 billion. Since then, Mojang has been responsible for keeping Minecraft alive and thriving by regularly releasing major updates that add new biomes, blocks, mobs, and gameplay features that keep the game fresh and exciting. They've expanded Minecraft across multiple platforms – from PC and consoles to mobile devices – making it accessible to millions of players worldwide.Their commitment to listening to player feedback and continuously improving the game has made Minecraft one of the best-selling and most beloved games of all time. Without Mojang's dedication to development and community support, Minecraft probably wouldn't have remained relevant for over a decade; instead, they've turned it into a cultural phenomenon that keeps growing stronger.
